FRM Level 1 Training Program

Book 1 - Module 1, 2 and 3

This introductory session provides coverage of fundamental risk management concepts that will be discussion in much more details throughout the FRM program. For the examination point of view, it is important to understand the general risk management process and its potential shortcomings, the concept of expected loss, and some of the underlying points regarding the relationship between risk and reward. This discussion will evaluate some advantages and disadvantages of hedging risk exposure and explain challenges that can arise when implementing a hedging strategy. This topic focuses on corporate governance, which is how companies operate, and include the roles of shareholders, senior managers, and the board of directors.
